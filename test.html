Visiting the SLAF Museum

The museum is open to the public on all days except Mondays and public holidays. Visitors can explore aircraft displays, aviation history, and exhibits of the Sri Lanka Air Force.

Important Visitor Guidelines

The museum compound is clearly demarcated and restricted areas are marked. Visitors must not enter unauthorized zones.

Photography and videography may require prior approval. These rules ensure national security and the safety of all visitors.
