Museum Galleries
MAIN HANGAR
Vintage Aircraft
De Havilland Tiger Moth • Chipmunk TMK.10 • SLAF manufactured aircraft
Collection of vintage aircraft used by SLAF since inception. Fully restored flying legends. Also houses four additional hangars with picture galleries, vehicles, arms, ordinance, uniforms and a mini-cinema.
OUTDOOR EXHIBITS
Aircraft & Artillery
LTTE aircraft shot down by SLAF • Wreckage & vehicles • 130mm howitzer
Positioned on professionally landscaped area adjacent to hangar complex. Testimony to dedication and surgical precision of the Sri Lanka Air Force.
HANGAR NO. 01
Aviation History
Photos depicting past & present of SLAF
Houses photos depicting the past and present of the SLAF and the aviation history of Sri Lanka.
HANGAR NO. 02
General Exhibits
Linik simulator • Right Flyer • Air defence guns • Air Force flags
General exhibits which have seen utility in the SLAF during the past years. Features the linik simulator model, right flyer, air defence guns and various flags of Air Force units.
HANGAR NO. 03
Civil Engineering
Complete civil engineering exhibits
The No 3 Hangar is contained with all the Civil Engineering exhibits, showcasing infrastructure development and engineering heritage of SLAF.
